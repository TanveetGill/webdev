The Chocolate Chip Muffin Recipe!

Today we will be learning how to make chocolate chip muffins

Ingredients

Everyone knows we can't make muffins without the right ingredients!

What you will need:

  1. all-purpose flour
  2. light-brown sugar
  3. baking powder
  4. milk and butter
  5. eggs and vanilla
  6. milk chocolate chips

Tools

Everyone knows we can't make muffins without the right tools either!

What you will need:

  1. Spoons
  2. Bowls
  3. Blender
  4. Baking Tray

Directions

Now we start baking, follow the step by step instructions carefully!

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C)

  2. Step 2

    3. In a bowl, combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, chocolate chips, and salt. Mix them well!

  3. Step 3

    4. In another bowl, combine milk, oil and egg. Blend them well!

  4. Step 4

    5. Fill cups 2/3 full. Sprinkle tops of muffins before baking with a combination of 3 tablespoons sugar and 2 tablespoon brown sugar.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 1 minute before removing from pan. Serve warm.
